Our story

Art from the heart of Bihar

Madhubani art has been passed down through generations of women in the Mithila region of Bihar. What began as wall paintings to celebrate festivals and life events has grown into a vibrant artistic tradition recognised worldwide.

At ecomm-site, we work directly with these artists — no middlemen, no mass production. Every painting, every saree, every piece of home décor you see is made by hand using natural pigments and techniques that have not changed in centuries.

When you buy from us, you're not just buying a product. You're supporting a family, preserving a tradition, and bringing a piece of Bihar's soul into your home.
